I am a geologist working across igneous petrology, geochemistry, structural geology, magmatic mineralisation, sedimentology, carbonate geology, climate geology, and remote sensing / GIS. My doctoral work at Periyar University examined the petrology, geochemistry, and tectonic settings of dolerite dykes of the Salem block within the Southern Granulite Terrane of Tamil Nadu, with implications for their petrogenesis. Earlier, my M.Phil. thesis at the University of Madras integrated shoreline, geomorphology, and land use/cover analysis along the Kovalam–Mahabalipuram coast, and my M.Sc. dissertation reconstructed palaeoenvironmental conditions of the Periyakurichchi Member of the Niniyur Formation in the Cauvery Basin. I work with petrological and reflected-light microscopy, GPR, GPS, Brunton compass, and thin-section preparation, and use GCDkit, R, Python, MATLAB, ArcGIS, QGIS, and SNAP for analysis.
